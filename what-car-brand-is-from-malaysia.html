Isuzu

If you are planning to buy the Isuzu model in Malaysia, it is better to choose the car from the local dealers. The national car dealership for Isuzu in Malaysia is Isuzu Malaysia. The local dealers provide warranty for the used and new car models on all the mechanical and electrical components within the car. They also provide a free car checkup service every 6 months to prevent the car from getting in serious trouble.

Mitsubishi

Mitsubishi is a Japanese car manufacturer headquartered in Tokyo, Japan. It was founded in 1926 by Kiichiro Sumimoto as a motorcycle and automobile motorcycle company. The company has a global presence and is present in over 140 countries. Mitsubishi is the fourth-largest car manufacturer in Japan and the seventh-largest in the world.

Conclusion

Yes, you’re seeing a lot of the same brands here as you would in other countries. But you’ll notice something unique about Malaysian car brands – they’re all maintained by the government! New car projects are developed by the government, and are then handed over to a private company to build. The government also helps finance these car projects. Not only does this help the government maintain a control over the quality of the cars produced, it also means that they are more affordable!
